About PICT

Overview

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT), established in 1983, is a premier engineering college affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University. Renowned for its student-centered approach and research-intensive environment, PICT fosters innovation and academic excellence.

Mission & Vision

PICT aims to nurture talent and encourage students to reach their full potential through a culture that promotes critical thinking and collaboration. The institute is committed to delivering world-class education in engineering and technology.
